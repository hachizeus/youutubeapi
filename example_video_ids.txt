How to find a YouTube video ID:
1. Go to a YouTube video
2. Look at the URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID
3. The part after "v=" is the video ID

For example, in https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
The video ID is: dQw4w9WgXcQ
